Understanding Your GST
📥 Output Tax (Collected)
GST you charge on every sale invoice at {{ gst_rate }}%. Customer pays this — you hold it for govt.
🔄 Input Tax Credit (ITC)
GST your suppliers charged on your purchases. You deduct this from what you owe.
🏛️ Net Payable to Govt
Output Tax − ITC = Amount to pay via GST portal. File GSTR-3B monthly.
